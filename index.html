About Me

Samantha is an actress, singer and dancer... and dog lover who enjoys playing the ukulele! Samantha studied in all areas of the performing arts at the Italia Conti Academy for three years, gaining a Level 6 Diploma in Professional Performing Arts, before going onto study a year of screen acting at the MN Academy of Film and Theatre.

Prior to attending Italia Conti, Samantha had a variety of performing arts credits. In 2008, she performed in her first West End show, the Take That Musical 'Never Forget' at the Savoy Theatre, West End, and that's when it all began! She was then chosen to tour the UK in the same musical in 2009. Since then, she has performed at the O2 Arena, was a dancer on Blue Peter (Series 54), was a singer in the United Nations CD Single, and was choreographer and dancer for the London 2012 Olympic Mascot Dance, where it was performed and filmed at the Piccadilly Theatre, West End.

And then training started at Italia Conti! Whilst studying, Samantha was introduced to Acting for Camera. She really excelled and loved getting in front of the camera! So much so, that she was offered lots of professional filming work, starting with the Universal Pictures feature film, Legacy, where she played the role of Lucy. Samantha also filmed two music videos, was a model for Converse and Greystar Student Living, was featured in the BBC London News regarding Max Whitlock, and was chosen to play multiple characters in E4's The Midnight Beast (Series 2).

The years flew by and before she knew it, it was Graduation Day! Since graduating, Samantha played Three, the supporting lead in the Award Winning Short Film, Extra Time, and then had leading roles in three more short films, as well as shooting a commercial. Samantha performed as a lead dancer, singer and swing in Warner Leisure's original musical, 'Gotta Sing, Gotta Dance!'. And she was back in the West End in 2016, performing in the musical, The Secret Garden, at the Ambassadors Theatre as the lead dancer, singer and puppeteer.
